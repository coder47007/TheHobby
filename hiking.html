Hiking

Hiking in Alberta is an exhilarating experience, offering some of the most scenic and diverse trails in Canada. From the towering peaks of the Canadian Rockies to the peaceful valleys and alpine meadows, Alberta's landscapes provide hikers with a variety of terrains and breathtaking views. Popular destinations like Banff and Jasper National Parks feature world-class trails that range from easy strolls to challenging multi-day treks. Hikers can explore rugged mountain ridges, walk along crystal-clear lakes, and pass through dense forests while encountering abundant wildlife, including elk, bears, and mountain goats.
